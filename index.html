How Scoring Works:

Wealth Score: Your share of total game wealth.

Civic Score: Your share of total civic contribution.

Commonwealth Score: (Wealth + Civic)

Note: scores normalized for easy comparison across game sizes.

Target Scores:

• 0-50: Below average
• 50-100: Average player
• 100-150: Strong player
• 150-200: Winning position

Victory Conditions:

Dominant Victory: If after 30 days one player has a score 75 points higher than the average across all players, they win.

Year-End Victory: Highest score on Sept 1 (day 365)

Strategy: Balance wealth and civic contributions. Pure wealth or pure civic strategies are very hard to win with!
